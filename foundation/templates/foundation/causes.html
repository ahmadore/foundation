Our Causes

Some of the functions of the Foundation include

EDUCATION

Purchase and distribution of admission forms to Agaie Emirate indigenes. Securing for admission into Tertiary Institutions as well as in the Armed Forces. Bursary assistance to students of higher Institutions.

HEALTH

Anti Malaria campaign, Action Against HIV/AIDS, Polio Eradication. Protection of Children Against Diseases. Assistance and Control of Epidemics and Health Education.

AGRICULTURE

Provision of fertilizer and Agro-Chemicals to small scale farmers. Market promotion of produce, Encouragement of Irrigation projects and Agric Education.
