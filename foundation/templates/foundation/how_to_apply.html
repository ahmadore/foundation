QUALIFICATIONS

The following are qualified to apply, Students of:

  • a. Universities.
  • b. Polytechnics.
  • c. Colleges of Education.
  • d. School of Nursing.
  • e. Other Tertiary institution.
  • f. Secondary School Leavers in the last 2 years with 4 credits and above.

REQUIREMENTS

The following should accompany duly completed application forms:

  • a. Letter of Admission.
  • b. Statement of result (Last Semester).
  • c. Letter of introduction by HOD/Students Affair Officer.
  • d. Receipts of payments (fees).
  • e. Letter of indigineship.
  • f. GCE/SSCCE/NECO result (Secondary School Leavers).
  • g. JME result (Secondary School Leavers).
